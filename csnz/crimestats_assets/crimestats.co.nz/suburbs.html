Frequently Asked Questions

How many suburbs are covered on CrimeStats.co.nz?⌄

We track crime statistics for all suburbs across New Zealand, covering every region from Northland to Southland.

How often is the crime data updated?⌄

Crime statistics are updated monthly using official New Zealand Police data and the latest population estimates.

Can I compare multiple suburbs?⌄

Use the comparison tool to compare up to 3 suburbs side-by-side, including crime rates, trends, and offence types.

What crime types are included?⌄

Burglary, theft, assault, fraud, sexual offences, robbery, and other offences as defined by NZ Police.

How do I find my suburb?⌄

Search using the box at the top, browse by region, or view the full alphabetical list.

Where does the crime data come from?⌄

All crime data comes from official New Zealand Police statistics and open data releases.
